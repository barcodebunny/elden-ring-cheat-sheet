Achievement Checklists

  • Legendary Armaments: Acquire all legendary armaments
  • Legendary Ashen Remains: Acquire all legendary ashen remains
  • Legendary Sorceries and Incantations: Acquire all legendary sorcery and incantations
  • Legendary Talismans: Acquire all legendary talismans
  • Ending Achievements: Achievements for different endings
  • Boss Achievements: Achievements for defeating bosses
  • Misc Achievements: Various other achievements

Legendary Armaments: Acquire all legendary armaments

  • Grafted Blade Greatsword: Reward for defeating Lionine Misbegotten in Castle Morne
  • Marais Executioner's Sword: Reward for defeating Elemer in the Shaded Castle
  • Eclipse Shotel: Found on the church altar of Castle Sol
  • Sword of Night and Flame: Found by dropping off of a connecting bridge in Caria Manor
  • Devourer's Scepter: Dropped by Bernahl at Warmaster's Shack when killed
  • Golden Order Greatsword: Reward for clearing Cave of the Forlorn
  • Ruins Greatsword: Reward for defeating boss duo in Redmane Castle
  • Bolt of Gransax: Climb the spear piercing through the city in Leyndell, the Royal Capital
  • Dark Moon Greatsword: Reward for completing Ranni's questline, and securing the Age of the Stars ending

Legendary Ashen Remains: Acquire all

  • Lhutel the Headless: Found in Tombsword Catacombs, west of the Minor Erdtree in Weeping Peninsula
  • Black Knife Tiche: Reward for clearing Ringleader's Evergaol in the Northwest of Moonlight Plateau
  • Redmane Knight Ogha: Reward for clearing War-Dead Catacombs inside Radahn's battle arena
  • Mimic Tear: Found in a chest in Night's Sacred Ground, behind a stonesword key gate
  • Ancient Dragon Knight Kristoff: Found in Sainted Hero's Grave, West of Leyndell, the Royal Capital
  • Cleanrot Knight Finlay: Found near the Prayer Room Site of Grace in Elphael

Legendary Sorceries and Incantations: Acquire all

    Sorceries

  • Founding Rain of Stars: Found in Heretical Rise in East Mountaintops of the Giants
  • Ranni's Dark moon: Found in Chelona's Rise in the Moonlight Altar Plateau, after killing three turtles in the area
  • Comet Azur: Found in Mt. Gelmir's Primeval Sorcerer Azur Site of Grace
  • Stars of Ruin: Received as part of Sellen's questline

    • Incantations

  • Flame of the Fell God: Reward for clearing Malefactor Evergaol in South Liurnia
  • Elden Stars: Found in Deeproot Depths, in a cave full of Gigantic Ants
  • Greyoll's Roar: Kill Greyoll, then purchase from Cathedral of Dragon Communion

Legendary Talismans: Acquire all

  • Radagon Icon: Found in Raya Lucaria. From the Debate Parlor Site of Grace, step outside then immediately head right
  • Radagon Soreseal: Found in Fort Faroth. Visible upon entering the fort. The room it resides in can be dropped down into from the catwalk above it
  • Godfrey Icon: Reward for clearing Golden Lineage Evergaol in South Altus Plateau
  • Moon of Nokstella: Found in chest under the giant throne in highest section of West Nokstella
  • Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman: Found in a chest close to Elphael's Drainage Channel Site of Grace
  • Marika's Soreseal: Found in Elphael near Prayer Room Site of Grace, behind a stonesword key gate
  • Old Lord's Talisman: Found in Farum near the Great Bridge Site of grace
  • Erdtree's Favor +2: Found in Leyndell, Ashen Capital, near the Forbidden Lands Site of Grace, down an elevator

Golden Seeds

Limgrave

  • Dropped by Ulcerated Tree Spirit in Fringefolk Hero's Grave.
  • Found after passing under the gates at Stormgate and fighting off the enemies and the troll there, you'll come up a hill along the path and happen upon a clearing. On your right will be the seed.
  • Given to you by Roderika after providing Chrysalid's Momento
  • Found in Mistwood, right in front of the entrance to Fort Haight.
  • Dropped by Kenneth Haight, if killed.

Stormveil Castle

  • Found on the castle grounds, near to where you meet Nepheli. Hard to miss if progressing through the castle.
  • Reward for defeating the miniboss at the bottom of Stormveil Castle.

Weeping Penninsula

  • South of the Callu Baptismal Church, on the other side of a giant wall.

Liurnia of the Lakes

  • Found in Academy Gate Town, near a glowing tree.
  • Found by following the road from Main Academy Gate northeast.
  • Found West of Raya Lucaria Academy's courtyard.
  • Found under a phantom tree in the upper sections of Caria Manor.

Nokstella, Eternal City

  • After acquiring the Moon of Nokstella, side exit on the south side, following a downward path to and elevator.

Siofra River

  • North-Northwest of Worshipper's Woods Site of Grace.

Caelid

  • Found on the road leading up to Beastial Sanctum.

FAQ

This cheat sheet was heavily adapted from the work by Stephen McNabb on their Dark Soul 2 Cheat Sheet source code, and Zackary Kjellberg's Dark Souls 3 Cheat Sheet.

Much of the information here was adopted from the fextralife Elden Ring Wiki, and PowerPyx Elden Ring guides.

There are things missing!

Apologies! This was mostly created for my own use, but I definitely saw benefit in making this public. I'm really trying to consolidate all collectible information into one place, however, this game is HUUUGGEE and it's a lot of work to track down every little piece I may be missing. If you'd like to contribute toward filling in any of the existing gaps, I'd surely appreciate it!

As of 03/08/2022, I know that a lot of links to the wiki are broken, or simply not present at all. I'll try to work on that, though it's not a huge priority of mine, compared to simply having the items able to be checked off.

I have feedback, how can I contribute?

You can visit the GitHub repository and report Issues or create a fork and submit a Pull Request.

Can I use this for multiple characters?

Yes! The profile selector in the Options tab will allow you to set up discrete profiles with their own progress.

How does the checklist status get saved?

The checklist is saved to your browser's local storage. Be careful when clearing your browser's cache as it will also destroy your saved progress.
